Parent Homework Helper

Get a parent-friendly explanation of any Grade R–7 CAPS homework question. So you can help your child, even if school was a long time ago.

We don't store your questions. Each one is processed and forgotten.

How to use this well. This tool explains the concept so you can teach your child. It deliberately doesn't give a copy-and-paste answer — that wouldn't help anyone. Read the explanation, then sit with your child and walk through it together.
